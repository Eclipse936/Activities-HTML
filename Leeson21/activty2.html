They belong to the Psittacidae family and are found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world. Fish are fascinating aquatic animals that live in rivers, lakes, seas, and oceans. They come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, from tiny goldfish to giant whale sharks. Most fish breathe through gills, which allow them to extract oxygen from water, and they use fins to swim gracefully. They play a vital role in maintaining the balance of aquatic ecosystems and are also an important source of food for humans and other animals. Besides their ecological importance, fish add beauty to our world and remind us of the wonders hidden beneath the water’s surface.
